Crepes

This strawberry crepe recipe has been a family favorite for over 30 years! These crepes are delicious and very rich! Be sure you have at least 1 hour to prepare them; they are worth every minute!

Ingredients:

  • ¾ cup all-purpose flour
  • ½ cup milk
  • ½ cup water
  • 3 eggs

Steps:

  1. Gather all your ingredients.
  2. Place flour, milk, water, eggs, melted butter, and salt into a blender; blend until smooth and set aside.
  3. Beat confectioners' sugar, cream cheese, lemon juice, lemon zest, and vanilla with an electric mixer in a large bowl until smooth.
  4. Gently fold in whipped cream.
